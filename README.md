# MRCOG-1 Clinical Management Flashcards

A static flashcard site for the MRCOG Part 1 **Clinical Management** module, covering
Maternal Medicine, Infections, Gynaecology, Labour & Postpartum, Labour & Multiple
Pregnancy, and 2025 Recalls.

**Live site:** _(GitHub Pages URL — added after deploy)_

## Using it
- **Browse**: scroll/search the cards; tap a card to reveal the answer.
- **Study**: flip through one card at a time (tap to flip; ← / → to move; space to flip), with shuffle.
- **Filter** by topic, search text, and by verification status.

## About the `⚠ AI` flag
Cards were generated by AI from lecture transcripts and checked against current
RCOG / NICE / BASHH / FSRH guidance. A card marked **`⚠ AI · verify`** either
conflicts with, is outdated per, or could not be confirmed against a guideline; the
answer shown is the AI's best guideline-aligned correction, with a note explaining
why. **Always verify flagged cards against the primary guideline.** This is study
support, not a clinical or exam authority.

## Files
- `index.html` — the app (vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, no build step)
- `cards.js` — the card data (`window.CARDS`)
